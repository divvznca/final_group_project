About us

“You are good boy, and I’m going to miss you at work.”
“I’m going to send this mail out and come play with you in a minute”

Sounds Familiar? We say this our this to our dogs too everyday and as pet parents we all worry about our dogs feeling lonely. Dogs as descendants of wolves are pack animals and inherently social. They love being with us and also their own kind. Pawz is our way of helping dogs make their own pack.
